MHuatt — AI-Powered Financial Literacy Platform

An AI-powered financial literacy platform built during the Borneo Hackwknd. MHuat helps users learn, plan, and manage their finances smarter through intelligent insights, spending analysis, and financial education. It integrates savings management, BNPL calculators, investment learning modules, insurance analysis, and retirement planning into one mobile application. Built with Flutter, Firebase, and Gemini AI.

Flutter
Firebase

Key Features

  • 🤖 AI Financial Assistant (Gemini AI chatbot for personalized guidance)
  • 📊 Smart Spending & BNPL Analysis with AI receipt scanning (ML Kit OCR)
  • 💰 Retirement Planning & Financial Goals tracker
  • 📈 Investment Learning & Portfolio Setup based on risk profile
  • 🛡 Insurance Analysis & Coverage Simulation with Document AI
